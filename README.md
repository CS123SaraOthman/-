# Healing the Wounds of Mountains (ترميم جروح الجبال) 🌱

**Transforming abandoned quarries into sustainable, productive lands by filling them with solid waste, capturing methane for energy, and restoring soil for agriculture and biodiversity.**

---

## 🏆 Achievements
- 4th place, West Bank Science Competition (Intel ISEF affiliate)  
- Qualified for international participation in the United States  

---

## 💡 Project Idea
At 16, I conducted this environmental research inspired by two major challenges in Palestine: abandoned stone quarries leaving deep scars, and random waste dumps.  

The project proposes an integrated solution:  
- Use solid waste to safely fill empty quarries  
- Extract methane gas from the waste for clean energy  
- Cover quarries with soil suitable for agriculture, allowing trees to be replanted and restoring biodiversity  

---

## 🔬 Work Conducted
- Field visits and consultation with local experts  
- Design of a physical model simulating the proposed system  
- Analysis of environmental impact and energy extraction potential  

---

## ⚙️ Features
- Quarry reclamation using solid waste  
- Methane gas extraction for renewable energy  
- Soil restoration and agriculture support  
- Biodiversity recovery
